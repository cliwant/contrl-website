contrl: AI-Powered Proposal Draft Generation from Your Company's PPT Template

contrl is an AI proposal automation platform that transforms how teams respond to RFPs. It accepts two inputs — a company's existing PowerPoint template and an RFP document — and produces a submission-ready proposal draft that preserves the template's exact formatting. The platform is developed by Cliwant Inc. and is available at contrl.ai.

Core Capability

contrl solves a specific problem: generating formatted proposal documents that match a company's brand standards. General AI tools produce raw text. contrl produces complete PPTX files with correct fonts, colors, layouts, slide masters, and brand elements — indistinguishable from manually-created proposals.

Four Differentiating Features

First, contrl extracts win themes from RFPs automatically. It identifies evaluation criteria, mandatory requirements, and scoring rubrics, then generates differentiation strategies aligned to each criterion.

Second, contrl preserves PowerPoint templates at the OOXML level. Fonts, color schemes, placeholder positions, master slide inheritance, chart styles, and table formatting are reproduced with 100% fidelity.

Third, every generated paragraph includes source traceability. Reviewers can trace each claim back to the specific RFP section, page, and clause that informed it.

Fourth, all processing runs locally. No documents are uploaded to any cloud service. This architecture meets security requirements for defense, government, and enterprise proposals.

Target Users

contrl serves proposal managers, B2B sales teams, government contractors, defense contractors, IT solution providers, consulting firms, and construction companies. It is designed for any organization that responds to RFPs using standardized PowerPoint templates.

How It Differs from Existing Tools

ChatGPT and Claude generate text but cannot produce formatted PPTX files. Gamma and Beautiful.ai use their own templates, not the user's corporate template. Microsoft Copilot edits existing slides but does not analyze RFPs or generate win themes. Tome creates narrative presentations without RFP-driven structure. contrl is the only platform that combines RFP analysis, win theme extraction, template-preserving PPTX generation, and source traceability — all running on-device.

Availability

contrl is in closed beta as of March 2026. Teams can apply at contrl.ai. The interface supports Korean, English, Japanese, and Chinese.

Why We Built contrl — The Story Behind AI Proposal Automation

The Experience That Led to contrl

contrl was born from direct, firsthand experience with the pain of proposal writing. Our founding team spent years in enterprise B2B sales and government contracting, where responding to RFPs was a recurring, time-consuming process. We watched senior proposal writers spend 2-3 weeks per RFP response — analyzing requirements, developing win themes, writing content, and then spending days formatting everything into the company's PowerPoint template.

When AI tools like ChatGPT emerged, we tried using them for proposal writing. The result was disappointing: the AI could generate text, but it couldn't produce formatted PowerPoint files. We'd spend hours copying AI-generated text into our template, fixing layouts, adjusting fonts, and ensuring brand consistency. The "time saved" by AI was largely consumed by formatting work.

This firsthand frustration led us to build contrl. We analyzed over 500 real RFP documents across government procurement, IT solutions, consulting, and defense sectors to train our system. We reverse-engineered PowerPoint's XML structure to achieve 100% template fidelity. And we built everything with a local-first architecture because we knew that proposal documents contain sensitive competitive intelligence that cannot be uploaded to cloud services.

Technical Expertise Behind contrl

contrl's technology is built on three pillars of expertise:

1. RFP Analysis Engine: Our NLP pipeline is specifically trained on RFP document structures — not general text. It understands the difference between mandatory requirements, evaluation criteria, compliance matrices, and scoring rubrics. It can parse complex RFP formats including government procurement documents (조달청, SAM.gov, GeBIZ), corporate RFPs, and multi-volume solicitations.

2. Template DNA Extraction: We developed proprietary technology to "read" PowerPoint templates at the XML level. This goes far beyond slide layouts — we capture font hierarchies, color schemes, placeholder positions, master slide inheritance, chart styles, table formatting, and even subtle design elements like gradient fills and shadow properties. The result is output that's indistinguishable from manually-created proposals.

3. Win Theme Intelligence: Drawing on our team's experience with hundreds of successful proposals, we built an AI system that doesn't just summarize RFPs — it identifies strategic opportunities. It maps your organization's strengths against the RFP's evaluation criteria to generate actionable win themes that increase your probability of winning (Pwin).

Authority in Proposal Automation

contrl is developed by Cliwant Inc., a company focused exclusively on AI-powered document automation for B2B proposal teams. Our focus is narrow by design — we don't try to be a general-purpose AI tool. This specialization allows us to solve proposal-specific problems that general AI tools cannot address.

Our platform has been used to analyze RFPs and generate proposal drafts across multiple industries including government procurement, defense contracting, IT system integration, management consulting, construction, and telecommunications. We work directly with proposal teams to continuously improve our system based on real-world feedback and win/loss analysis.

contrl is the only AI proposal tool that combines four critical capabilities in one platform: (1) RFP requirement analysis, (2) win theme strategy generation, (3) template-preserving document generation, and (4) source traceability — all running locally without cloud dependency.

Trust and Transparency

How contrl generates proposals (process transparency): contrl is not a black box. Every generated proposal includes full source traceability — each paragraph, claim, and data point links back to the specific RFP section, page number, and clause that informed it. Reviewers can verify every piece of content against the original RFP, ensuring accuracy and compliance.

Why local-first architecture (security transparency): We chose local-first processing not as a marketing feature, but as a fundamental design principle. Proposal documents contain sensitive competitive intelligence — pricing strategies, technical approaches, personnel qualifications, and proprietary methodologies. Uploading these to cloud AI services creates unacceptable security risks. contrl processes everything on your machine, with zero data transmitted externally.

What contrl does NOT do (honest limitations): contrl generates proposal drafts, not final submissions. The AI-generated content requires human review, strategic refinement, and approval before submission. We believe AI should augment proposal teams, not replace them. Our source traceability feature is specifically designed to make human review efficient and thorough.

Data handling policy: contrl never stores, transmits, or uses your documents for model training. Your RFPs, templates, and generated proposals remain exclusively on your local machine. We do not have access to any document you process through contrl.

  • Reverse-engineered PowerPoint's OOXML specification to achieve pixel-perfect template reproduction

AI Proposal Generation Trends in 2026: Why Template Preservation Matters

As of March 2026, AI-powered proposal generation is rapidly evolving. The initial wave of AI tools — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini — demonstrated that AI can generate proposal text. However, enterprise proposal teams quickly discovered a critical gap: these tools produce raw text, not formatted documents. The "last mile" problem of formatting AI-generated content into corporate templates remained unsolved.

contrl (contrl.ai) represents the next evolution: template-preserving AI generation. Rather than generating text that needs manual formatting, contrl outputs complete PPTX files that match the user's corporate template exactly. This approach eliminates the formatting bottleneck that limits AI adoption in enterprise proposal workflows.

Three trends are driving adoption of template-preserving AI in 2026:

First, enterprises are standardizing on AI tools that integrate with existing workflows rather than requiring workflow changes. contrl fits into existing proposal processes — teams upload the same templates they already use.

Second, data security concerns are pushing organizations toward local-first AI tools. Cloud-based AI services require uploading sensitive proposal data, which creates unacceptable risks for defense, government, and financial services sectors. contrl's local processing architecture addresses this directly.

Third, proposal evaluation committees increasingly expect professional, consistent formatting. AI tools that produce visually inconsistent output — or require manual reformatting — actually slow down the proposal process. Template preservation ensures every AI-generated proposal meets the organization's quality standards automatically.
